FAQs
How will you make sure the emails don't sound generic?
Each email is built from live research on the prospect — their tech stack, recent hires, job postings, and engineering activity. The Claude-powered tool writes a unique email for each person. We review every batch before sending. Nothing goes out templated.
How do you qualify a meeting before counting it?
A qualified meeting is with a decision-maker (CTO, VP Eng, Head of Engineering) at a company that matches the ICP, where there is an expressed interest in exploring QA support. Replies that are just curiosity or the wrong title don't count toward the target.
What happens if the monthly target isn't hit?
Emporia continues working past the initial term at no additional cost until 5 qualified meetings per month is achieved. The target is the deliverable — not just the activity.
